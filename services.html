Services

Green Thumb Landscape Management provides full-service exterior maintenance for commercial, institutional and community properties throughout Sarasota and Manatee Counties. Our programs prioritize consistency and lasting landscape performance managed by experienced, locally trained crews.

Grounds Maintenance

A well-managed landscape is the foundation of a professional property. Our maintenance programs are customized for regional growth patterns and proactive care. Crews are trained to uphold appearance standards while minimizing disruption to daily operations.

Core Services:

  • Scheduled turf care and edging
  • Bed detailing and selective pruning
  • Shrub and canopy management
  • Seasonal cleanups and debris control

Fertilization & Weed Control

Soil composition and climate dictate how Florida landscapes perform. We apply calibrated treatments that support healthy growth while controlling unwanted species. Each program is reviewed regularly to maintain soil balance, plant health, and overall landscape integrity.

Core Services:

  • Soil testing and nutrient calibration
  • Targeted weed management
  • Turf and ornamental fertilization
  • Seasonal treatment planning

Annual Plantings

Our team designs and installs annual rotations using regionally suited plant material that thrives in Florida's unique ecosystem. Our services reinforce brand image and visual appeal with year round accuracy.

Core Services:

  • Annual flower rotations
  • Bed redesign and enhancement
  • Mulch and rock application
  • Accent and entryway plantings

Property Enhancements

Strategic enhancements strengthen both appearance and asset value. We develop and execute improvement plans that modernize existing landscapes, enhance functionality and reinforce site identity across large portfolios.

Core Services:

  • Entrance and frontage improvements
  • Plant replacement and bed upgrades
  • Hardscape and lighting additions
  • Amenity and common-area renovations

Tree & Palm Care

Professional arbor care ensures your people, property and environment are protected. We provide structured pruning and maintenance programs that are suited to Florida's native and ornamental species.

Core Services:

  • Structural pruning and canopy shaping
  • Palm cleaning and height maintenance
  • Hazard assessment and corrective trimming
  • Storm preparation and debris removal

Irrigation Management

Irrigation management is essential for your landscape and overall costs. Our water management specialists monitor and fine-tune systems to ensure optimal distribution and effective conservation in compliance with all local regulations.

Core Services:

  • System inspections and audits
  • Controller calibration and scheduling
  • Leak detection and repair
  • Water efficiency reporting

Hurricane Response & Recovery

In Florida, hurricane preparation and recovery is essential for property resilience. Our hurricane response programs secure landscapes proactively before impact, while restoring accessibility and function after a storm. We mobilize trained crews and equipment to manage recovery promptly.

Core Services:

  • Pre-storm assessments and readiness plans
  • Debris clearing and site stabilization
  • Replanting and restoration services
  • Erosion and drainage correction
