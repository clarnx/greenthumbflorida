FAQs

How do you determine the right maintenance program for a property?+

Each site begins with a detailed evaluation of landscape conditions, irrigation systems and maintenance requirements. From here, we develop a tailored scope of work that aligns with your standards, budget and long-term goals.

Can you manage multi-site or portfolio properties?+

Yes. Our structure supports clients with single locations as well as regional and multi-site portfolios. Dedicated account managers oversee scheduling, quality control and reporting across all properties to ensure consistency.

How does your team address Florida's seasonal challenges?+

We plan services around regional growth cycles and weather patterns. For example, we account for irrigation adjustments during dry months, as well as proactive storm preparation and clean up during Hurricane Season. Our programs are foundationally designed for our client's peace of mind. Our goal is to protect appearance and performance year round.

Are your crews licensed and insured?+

All Green Thumb Landscape Management professionals are fully licensed and insured in accordance with Florida regulations. Safety and compliance are fundamental to us.

What can property managers expect in terms of communication and oversight?+

Clients receive direct access to their account manager, proactive site walk reports and clear service documentation. Our end goal is transparency. Every detail of your landscape program is monitored, managed, and communicated before you even have to ask.
